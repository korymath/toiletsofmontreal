Toilets of Montreal

Flush with Creativity: Montreal's Discarded Toilets Celebrated in a Stunning Photography Collection

Toilets of Montreal is a collection of photographs of discarded toilets in public in Montreal, Canada. The book features over 100 photographs of toilets in a variety of settings, from busy subway stations to quiet parks. The photographs are accompanied by short essays that explore the history and culture of toilets en plein air in Montreal.

If you spot a toilet outside anywhere in the world, share it with Kory by email or at @toiletsofmontreal on Instagram.
